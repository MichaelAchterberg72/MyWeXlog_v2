Capture Education / Training - Help

 

This page displays the fields that are required to capture your Education and Training experience.

The fields required are:

Course Name This should be descriptive of the project so it is easily identifiable by people that are in collaboration companies as well. The drop-down items are made up of the course name, the institution name and the form of certification.
The Add a Course link will request you to add the course details such as the course name, the institution, the course type, the website as well as certification type.
Course Subject This will be selected from the dropdown list and should be descriptive of the subject in that course.
The Add a Topic link will request you to add brief details of the course subject or topic name, the skills learned as well as the number of hours spent in studies for the course.
Date from This will be input utilising the date widget that is accessible from the date widget that appears when you hover the mouse over the field. By clicking on the arrows on the right hand side of the date field and clicking them clicking on the year you can navigate to the date you require and you can select the date required.
Date to This works in the same way as the date from field but should be the date you have completed the course subject.
Upload This should be used to upload the course completion certificate or attendance certificate. Simply click the Choose File button and utilise the standard file explorer dialog box to select the file.
Comments This is available for you to enter any comments you have on the course.

**In the event that one of the dropdown lists is not populated please refresh the page.

A * next to the field name indicates that the field is a required field.

You have the option to:

  • Add Validators - allows you to navigate to the page which allows you to capture lecturers or classmates that will be requested to validate your attendance, skills learnt and time spent on the course.
