Experience Home Page - Education & Training - Help

 

The experience is listed in date order with, in the 'Education and Training', the 'Start Date, 'Completion Date', Course' and 'Time Logged', and in the 'Eork Experience' and 'Previously Logged Experience' the 'Employer, 'Date from', 'Date to', 'Hours' and 'Project' data items with the most recent date first.

The ‘Education and Training’ card contains an abbreviated list of the five most recent relevant captured experience.

You are able to open the full list by clicking on the 'View All Education' button as required. This button will only be visible once you have more than five items in the list.

  • You are able to add a new experience entry by clicking on the ‘Add Education’ button at the top right of the experience card. Education and Training is self describing in that it is where you can capture all time spent studying or on course. Time logged against this is not counted in your overall experience rating.

The options you will be provided with will be:

Detail This will allow you to view the detail of the entry.
Remove This will allow you to delete an entry, there is no confimation step.

What can be Claimed as Experience

There are a number of activities that can be classified as experience, These are:

  1. Time spent studying at university or colledge
  2. Time spent studying on a course provided by your job
  3. Time spent "studying" at social club / sports club committees

Once experience has been captured you will need to tag skills used during that time.

This can include:

  • Government departments that were dealt with
  • Computer programs used
  • Laws being worked with
  • Case Law you worked with
  • Regulations you worked with
  • Codes and standards you worked with
  • Activities (report writing)
  • Skills (coding, meetings, research, etc.)
  • Commodities you worked with (Gold, platinum, coal, etc.)
  • Country where the work was performed (shows familiarity with customs etc.)
  • Many, many others...
