About Us!

Our goal is to provide our members with affordable experience tracking and quality vacancies.

Who we are

WexLog.io is a division of DevOpTec (Pty.) Ltd (ABN: 0000) a tech company formed in the territory of Queensland, Australia. WexLog began in the early months of 2019 and was officially launched in October of 2019.

James Keir is the CEO of the company and the management includes experienced executives with experience in programming, database administration, data security and business development.

WexLog is a supplemental portal to other portals aimed at professional people who capture their experience via static means: CV’s, etc.. We directly capture a person’s experience, provide a trust passport and provide access to international work prospects.

We directly quantify the experience of individuals, companies as well as for the people that work within them.

 

Mission

WexLog's mission is to create a portal that is aimed at allowing people to capture their experience and have it ratified or signed off by their clients / superiors / peers whilst providing a portal for vacancies.

WeXlog is designed to allow people who have been working for several years and have a large amount of experience that may not be able to be authorised as they no longer work with those clients or at the company any longer. The portal allows people to capture this time, however this is displayed as such.

The captured time will then be saved against their profile, available for people to search for that experience.

Once captured, clients requiring skills or services should perform a search and find people with the needed experience. The results would be displayed in terms of distance from the prospective client, experience and ratings.

 

The Problem

The working world is moving toward a gig economy at an increasing rate. In this economy, people offer their skills on a freelance basis, marketing their abilities through their networks. There are methods through which people can market their abilities available, however these methods are static and do not give an indication as to the level of experience a person has apart from the length of their working career.

These methods are also inconsistent in that a person that has over 10 years overall experience, in construction for example, but has only been involved in airport construction for 1 year does not easily get noticed.

Similarly, in the service industry, people do not know what experience technicians/mechanics etc. have when it comes to their particular needs. If you had an Audi diesel, you would want to identify the mechanic that has the most experience with that car as well as whether they offer a quality service.

In the service industry it is possible to rate a person’s service using platforms currently available, however, that rating may be made by a person that does not know your car. Indeed, the mechanic in question may be an expert on another model but know very little of yours.

In the professional services industry one can source skills from a global market, but the question remains: How do you find people with ratified experience in the field you require.

The concept of a timesheet is familiar with many people in the legal, engineering, accounting, and many other fields. But this system is designed to measure the amount of work performed for billing purposes.

By extending this system, one can add the knowledge and skill used in performing that work and build up a knowledge base which is approved by a client or superior, not simply added in an uncontrolled fashion.

 

The Solution

WexLog is designed to be a system that creates a trust passport of verified experience on a dynamic level capturing the training and education as well as the work experience skills.

No matter what industry you work in, you need to be able to quantify the skills that you developed over the lifespan of your career and provide a marketable portfolio that you will be able to send to your prospective clients, employers or colleagues to qualify who you are and what experience you have in the performance of your work.

The WexLog solution allows you to do that is a concise, dynamic method by capturing your experience in continual daily, weekly or bi-weekly updates. This ensures that you have a full portfolio available at the click of a button to apply for a vacancy or bid that is available.
