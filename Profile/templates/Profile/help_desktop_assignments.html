Desktop Assignments - Help

 

This details the assignments that have been authorised. The talent will have the authorised assignments listed and the employers will also have their authorised assignments listed.

The information listed will be the Vacancy Name, Company Branch and city as well as the Reference Number. The date assigned, Offered Rate, Assignment Start date and Assignment Completed date are also detailed.


Assignments as Talent

This will list your assignments that are current.

The options you will be provided with when validating experience will be:

Allows you to open and review the vacancy detail.
Allows you to accept the assignment, functional button only so does not open any informational pages.
Informational button only detailing that you have requested further information and as yet have not received it.
Request
Information		 Once the vacancy has been assigned you are able to request more detailed information. This will open a dialog box in which you will be able to detail the information required.
In the event that the assignment has been issued to you and you are unable to perform you will be able to decline the assignment. You will be required to select a reason from the drop down list and provide a detailed reason as to why the assignment is being decline.


Assignment as an Employer

This will list all the current vacancies that have been assigned and are in progress.

The options you will be provided with when validating experience will be:

Will be visible when the talent has requested more information.
Will be visible while awaiting the acceptance of the assignment by the talent.
Will be visible should the talent that the vacancy has been assigned to declines the assignment.
ReAssign
Vacancy		 Should the vacancy assignment be declined this button will be visible allowing you to reassign the position to another talent.
Will be visible and will indicate that the assignment has been accepted by the talent.
