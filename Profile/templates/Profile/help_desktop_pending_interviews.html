Desktop Pending Interviews - Help

 

This is broken down into two sections, Interviews Pending as a Candidate and Interviews Pending as an Employer.


Pending Interviews as a Candidate

This will list all the interviews that have been requested and accepted listing the vacancy, configuration, the reference number and invitation date.

The options you will be provided with when confirming an interview will be:

Shows the detail of the vacancy.
Allows you to accept the interview request. This will update the employers interview records to reflect that the interview has been accepted.
Once the button has been clicked the button will disappear.
Allows you to decline the the interview request, reasons are personal.
Interview
Completed		 Please select this option to update the system as to your having completed the interview and awaiting notification of assignment.
Once the button has been clicked the button will disappear.


Pending Interviews as an Employer

This lists the interviews that have been requested in the form of an interview request from a potential employer as well as on the side of the talent who submitted the proposal bid for any vacancies advertised.

The options you will be provided with when validating experience will be:

Visible when the talent has not yet accepted the request for an interview.
Visible when the talent has accepted the requested interview.
Interview Page Displays the interview page allowing you to capture notes from the interview.
Visible when the interviewee declined the request for an interview.
Removes a talent from the interview list and updates the candidates interview list.
