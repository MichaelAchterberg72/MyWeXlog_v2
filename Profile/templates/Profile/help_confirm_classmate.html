Confirm as a Classmate - Help

 

This details the validations that have been requested of you as a fellow classmate.

The information listed will be the name of the person who requested the validation from you, the course you attended with the company who provided it, the subject topic and the dates from and till that the talent studied the course with you.


Actions Required

The options you will be provided with when validating the experience will be:

Confirm the validation without requiring any further action. No comment or reference will be provided.
Reject the validation due to the person not knowing you or having not attended the course. This does not require any further action.
Review with Comment Confirm the experience and provide reference comments as to your experience of the person during the course.
Should you have received the validation request in error please select this option as it will cancel the request and notify the requester of their error.
