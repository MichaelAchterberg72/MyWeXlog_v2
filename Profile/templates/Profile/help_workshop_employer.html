Employer Workshop - Help

 

This is broken down into four sections, Open Assignments, Pending Assignments, Completed Assignments and Declined Assignments.


Section Details

Each of the sections displays information relating to then status of the assignments that have been issued to you. The information is divided into six areas:-

  • Vacancy Reference Number
  • Assignment assigned to
  • Status of task completion by the talent
  • Status of task completion by yourself
  • Successful pay rate for the assignment
  • Due date for the vacancy
  • Actions available for information relating to the assignment

The options you will be provided with for the assignment detail will be:

Additional Information Requested - Indicates that the talent has requested additional information prior to acceptance if the assignment.
Pending Status - Indicates that the vacancy acceptance is still pending.
Incomplete Completion checkmark - allows you to mark the assignment as complete by clicking on it.


Actions available for each assignment

This details the icons available under the Actions heading and what each of them will present.

The options you will be provided with will be:

Contact Detail - Provide you with the contact details of the member that has issued you with the assignment (Name, email address and link to their profile).
Vacancy Detail - Open the vacancy detail for which you applied.
Performance Rating - Allow you to rate the performance of the employer on four points with comments as to your experience whilst working for them.
