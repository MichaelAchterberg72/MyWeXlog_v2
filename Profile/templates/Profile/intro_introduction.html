MyWeXlog Reason for Being

"To advance humanity through the measurement of skills and knowledge and its effective application to solve the challenges that plague us."


MyWeXlog is a live experience log on which you can capture your experience and associate skills with that experience. Then, depending on your subscription, you can either search for companies looking for your skills or find people that have skills you require.

An Introduction

MyWeXlog is designed around its central feature, the experience log, and the features that are derived from its compilation. The first freely accessible feature is the public profile, which is a confirmed log of your work history, your experience and skills gained from your working life over time, projects you were involved with, your weekly achievements and accolades gained including the confirmations of how you succeeded in the development of your career as it happened. We have attempted to remove all bias, such as gender, race, age, etc. out of the system by anonymizing the presentation of the information on MyWeXlog as much as we can.

By compiling a confirmed log of your experience and skills you create an authentic and trustworthy record of your career. This promotes your unique talents which can then be found by people and companies that require them.

MyWeXlog then builds on your confirmed experience and public profile and provides a marketplace in which you are able to find employment based on your experience.

How MyWeXlog Works

MyWeXlog works by capturing your skills and experience to make a verified log and then making it available to the people and companies requiring those skills.

The Workflow

Capture Your Profile

  1. Select 'View Profile' from the icon menu at the top right corner
  2. Capture your name and all relevant information as outlined in the cards
  3. Ensure you capture your current employment in the 'Career History' card as this information is required to populate the company section when creating a vacancy
  4. Your 'Background' and 'Motivation' will be used as default fields on you Public Profile

Capture Experience

  1. Capture weekly experience detailing your skills used and developed
  2. Request your co-workers to validate the experience
  3. Market your public profile on your CV, social media and other marketing media

Vacancies

  1. Mark yourself as available for vacancies
  2. Apply for or be selected for vacancies based on your skills and experience
  3. Get shortlisted
  4. Be invited for an interview
  5. If successful be assigned the advertised vacancy
