Confirm as a Colleague - Help

 

This details the validations that have been requested of you as a fellow colleague.

The information listed will be the name of the person who requested the validation from you, the company who you work for, the project on which you were working on, their designation, the dates from and till that the talent has requested validation from you, the number of hours they worked and the skills that the requester utilised in the performance of their work.


Actions Required

The options you will be provided with when validating the experience will be:

Allows you to confirm the validation without requiring any further action. No comment or reference will be provided.
Allows you to reject the validation due to the person not knowing you or having not worked with you. This does not requie any further action.
Confirm with Comment Allow you to confirm the experience and provide reference comments as to your experience of the person during the performance of their work.
Should you have received the validation request in error please select this option as it will cancel the request and notify the requester of their error.
