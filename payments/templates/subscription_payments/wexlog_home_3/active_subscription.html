What is the payment method?

We utilise PayPal as our payments partner so all accounts and payments are secured by their tried and tested methodologies and payments are easily managed through the PayPal accounts manager allowing you to amend or delete your debit order at your own requirement.

How do I know my payment info is secure?

PayPal has developed a secure world class payment solution that is trusted and used by more than 7 million businesses and 200 million individuals worldwide.

How do I know you wont charge more than agreed?

We only charge for the monthy, six monthly or yearly debit orders. Any additional services will be charged on an ad hoc basis as you use them.

How do I cancel my subscription?

We make use of PayPal as our billing partner so to cancel your subscription you will need to log-in to your PayPal account and navigate to the payments section. Once you found the correct item simply click on it and cancel the recurring payment.

Can I re-subscribe if I cancel my subscription?

MyWeXlog allows you to cancel your subscription if you are not using it, continue capturing your experience in the free subscription and then re-subscribe as either an active or a passive subscriber.

What if I delete my account?

In the event that you choose to delete your account all your data will be deleted from the MyWeXlog system. We recommend you save your data before you delete your account.
