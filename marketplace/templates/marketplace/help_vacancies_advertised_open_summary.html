This provides a brief list of the five most recent vacancies that you have listed as currently open.


Post Vacancy

Open the page that will allow you to capture a new vacancy.



The table lists the date posted, vacancy title, the closing date, the response which will reflect 'Yes' if members have replied to the vacancy submitting a bid and 'None' if no bids have been received as yet.

The options you will be provided with will be:

Shows the detail of the vacancy allowing you to edit the vacancy as well as a list of the talent suited to the vacancy and a list of applicants for the vacancy. You will have access to the vacancy interview and short lists.
Opens the interview details list specifically for the vacancy advertised. This will list all the interviews of talent for the position.

The following buttons will all you to view:

Interview History

Interview History

Open the page listing the paginated full list of all interviews that you have scheduled across all vacancies posted.

The talents response will indicate the following options:

  • Interview Pending
  • Accept Interview
  • Decline Interview

as well as whether or not you have responded to the interview and the interview status being either 'Open' or 'Closed'.

All Open Vacancies
