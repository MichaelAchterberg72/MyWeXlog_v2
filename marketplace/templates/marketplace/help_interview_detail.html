My Vacancies Advertised - Interview Detail - Help

 

This provides detail of the interview with a list of comments for each of the talent that has been interviewed for that vacancy.

It will display the vacancy title and system reference number at the top of the list.

Each of the talent interviews or scheduled for an interview will be listed with the talents alias, the talent's response to the interview request, your selection as to the talent's suitability, the interview status as well as the your comments from the interview.


Talent listed Option Buttons

The button options available are:

Open Allow you to easily close the interview status by clicking on the red cross.
Allow you to open and view the vacancy detail page.
Open the dialog page to allow you to add a comment for the interview.


View All Talent
