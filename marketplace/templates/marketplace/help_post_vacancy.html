Add or Edit a Vacancy - Help

 

This page displays the fields that are required to capture a new or edit vacancy.

The fields required are:

Vacancy Title Please enter the title for the vacancy making sure that it is reflective of the vacancy and position that it refers to.
Vacancy Reference Number Enter a unique reference number for the vacancy. This is the reference number that will be used in the event of performing a search.
Company Branch Type the company name in the search field at the top of the dropdown list that appears when you click on the field, select from the dropdown list the Company name and branch that you are wanting, if you add a new Company Branch it will automatically populate the field.
The Add a Company Branch link will open a popup window and request you to add the company, branch or division name, type, and physical address.
Hours Enter the number of hours the vacancy is expected to take for completion or alternatively the number of hours the company has budgeted for at the rate offered.
Unit Enter the unit type of hours required for the vacancy is expected to take for completion or alternatively the type of contract the company has budgeted for at the rate offered. The selection possibilities are once off, per month, per day, per week, short term contract and as and when contract.
Experience Level Select from the drop down list the experience level required for prospective tenders on the vacancy. The categories are:
  • Student (<=326 hours or up to 2 months of experience)
  • Beginner (<=2,282 hours or up to 14 months (two years) of experience)
  • Junior (<=12,062 hours or 62 months (five years) of experience)
  • Intermediate (<=25,754 hours or 134 months (eleven years) of experience)
  • Senior (<=45,314 hours or 254 months (twenty one years) of experience)
  • Lead ( more than 45,314 hours (more than twenty one years) of experience)
Currency Select the currency that you will be remunerating with from the dropdown list and if you add a currency that is not available ion the list it will automatically populate the field.
The Add a Currency link will open a popup window and request you to add the country, currency in full written format with country and currency name and the internationally recognised currency abbreviation like USD or AUD.
Rate Offered Enter the offered rate of remuneration to be paid.
Rate Unit Enter the offered rate unit to be paid. The categories are:
  • per Hour
  • per Day
  • per Month
  • Lump Sum
Work Type Configuration Enter the expected location limit required. The options are:
  • Local
  • Remote
City, Town or Place Select from the dropdown list and the localised location the company branch operates.
The Add a City, Town or place link will request you to add the Region and City, Town or Place name if your desired option is not in the list.
To be Completed By Here please enter the date that you required the job to be completed. Open the date picker by clicking on the up or down arrows that appear on the right of the capture field when you hover over it.
Vacancy Status Select whether or not the vacancy is either:
  • Open
  • Closed
Vacancy Closes Here please enter the date that vacancy offering for the job to be completed. Open the date picker by clicking on the up or down arrows that appear on the right of the capture field when you hover over it.
Certifications Required Please enter any certifications that will be required for the performance of the vacancy. You may start typing the name of the certificate and the search field of the drop down list will filter for what you are looking for and capture the required certificates in tags allowing more than one to be captured.
The Add a Certificate link will request you to add a certificate type if your desired option is not in the list.
Scope Here you are able to enter the scope for the vacancy offering to be completed. The field will allow you to enter paragraphs.
Expectations Here you are able to enter the expectations for the vacancy offering to be completed. The field will allow you to enter paragraphs.

**In the event that one of the dropdown lists is not populated please refresh the page.

A * next to the field name indicate that it is a required field.

Click the or button when complete.
