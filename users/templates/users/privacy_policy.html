Choose a Robust Email/Password Combination to Protect Account Access

MyWeXlog utilizes a number of computer security best practices and procedures to protect access to the personally identifiable data collected and stored at MyWeXlog. However, the most important access control starts with you. When you register for an account, MyWeXlog will prompt you to provide a unique and robust passphrase along with a unique username.

Your username and passphrase will serve as your login credentials. To prevent unauthorized access to your account, it’s important you select a username/passphrase combination that you have never used at any other website. Otherwise, if you’re using the same username/passphrase combination at MyWeXlog that you use elsewhere, and if that other website or service is later hacked and your login credentials stolen, it could allow those criminals to randomly try logging into other popular websites such as MyWeXlog to gain access to your account. As a user of the MyWeXlog service, please help us help you by selecting a robust and unique passphrase. We recommend using a password manager to assist in creating a unique passphrase. Bottom line, choose a passphrase at MyWeXlog that you have never used anywhere else. Should you wish to change your passphrase or have forgotten your passphrase please select the ‘Change Password’ link provided in the Icon menu at the top right of the page and follow the required procedures. Should you have forgotten your passphrase please click on the “Forgot Password’ link on the log-on page and follow the prompts.

In addition, we recommend that you configure multi-factor authentication upon account creation through your profile security settings.

By design, MyWeXlog limits the amount of personally identifiable information that could be stolen should a criminal gain access to your account. We do not store credit card numbers as this is handled by our payments partner PayPal. We also strive to avoid collecting information we don’t need.
